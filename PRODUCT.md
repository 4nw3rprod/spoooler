# Instagram Reel Generator

**Register:** product

## Product Purpose
A local internal tool to automate the creation of high-quality Instagram Reels. It handles the entire pipeline: 
1. Source selection (topic or URL)
2. Script drafting & media scraping
3. Layout pattern matching
4. AI voiceover generation
5. Local MP4 rendering via Remotion

## Users
Internal marketing teams and content creators who need to rapidly produce Instagram reels at scale without manual video editing. They are technical enough to run local apps but need a streamlined, dashboard-like experience.

## Brand Tone
Professional, efficient, and precise. The tool should feel like a high-end local macOS application (e.g. Linear, Raycast, or an "Operator UI") rather than a generic SaaS app. 

## Strategic Principles
- Function over decoration: prioritize the live video preview and execution logs.
- Minimal friction: fewer clicks to get a generated video.
- Clear state: users should instantly understand what stage of generation the app is in.
